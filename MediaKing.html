How It Works

From brief to trending in 4 steps

Our streamlined workflow means you get polished, platform-ready videos without the back-and-forth chaos. Fast turnaround, zero stress.

01

Share Your Footage & Brief

Upload your raw footage, reference videos, and creative direction. We review everything before starting to ensure we're fully aligned on vision.

02

We Edit & Craft Your Story

Our editor selects the best moments, writes the hook, paces the edit for retention, adds captions, music, and motion graphics — all on-brand.

03

Review & Revision Round

You receive your first draft within the agreed timeline. We include up to 2 rounds of revisions so every detail feels exactly right.

04

Delivered & Ready to Post

Final files are delivered in platform-optimal formats — 9:16 for TikTok/Reels, 1:1 for feeds, 16:9 for YouTube. Post and watch it perform.
